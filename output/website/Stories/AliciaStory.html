Alicia Story


Summary

A day of self-management by Alicia, who is an 18-year-old type 1 diabetic with a closed loop system.


Rationale

Examining the usability of the self-reported data standard


Story

Alicia is an 18-year-old type 1 diabetic who lives at home with her parents. She does not plan on going to university and is doing teacher training in her hometown.

She lives an active lifestyle, very rarely drinks and never smokes because of a passion for long distance running. Her mother used to help her with MDI, but now she is on a closed loop with a CGM and a pump. She has moved from paediatric care to adult care.

Alicia collects a day of self-reported data using her CGM and insulin pump. She also has an appointment with her GP to discuss how her self-management is going.
